AIML CLUB

The club teaches students about Machine learning. It creates a great environment for students to discuss and learn about various parts of ML and AI. The club also provides students with resources and guidance to explore specific fields of AIML.AIML stands for Artificial Intelligence Modelling Language. AIML is an XML based markup language meant to create artificial intelligent applications. AIML makes it possible to create human interfaces while keeping the implementation simple to program, easy to understand and highly maintainable.
